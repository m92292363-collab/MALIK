Welcome to Imam Malick Islamic Institute

Imam Malick Islamic Institute is dedicated to providing outstanding Islamic and academic education to the youth of The Gambia, combining authentic religious scholarship with modern knowledge.

Named in honour of Imam Malick ibn Anas, one of the greatest Islamic scholars, our institute upholds the tradition of rigorous scholarship and moral excellence.

OUR MISSION

  • TO PROVIDE ALL EDUCATIONALLY SERVICES AND INFRASTRUCTURAL FACILITIES BOTH TEACHERS AND STUDENTS TO OPERATE IN A CONDUCIVE TEACHING AND LEARNING ENVIRONMENT WITH AN OPTIMUM ACADEMIC AND PROFESSIONAL PERFORMANCE

OUR VISION

To be the foremost Islamic educational institute in The Gambia, producing graduates of outstanding character, knowledge and leadership.

We envision a community of scholars whose conduct reflects the noble legacy of Imam Malick — combining depth of faith with breadth of learning.

Through continuous improvement and community engagement, we strive to be a beacon of Islamic excellence in West Africa.

About Our Institute

Imam Malick Islamic Institute has been serving the community of The Gambia with quality Islamic and academic education. Our institute is built on the foundations of scholarly tradition, faith, and community service.

We strive to build and unlock the potential of our young generation in order to play a pivotal role in all spheres of national development.
